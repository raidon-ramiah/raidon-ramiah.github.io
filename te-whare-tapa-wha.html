Taha Tihana // Physical Wellbeing

I keep my physical wellbeing up by exercising four times a week. I like to also try to eat healthy most days.

Taha Whanau // Family Wellbeing

I have dinner with my family every night and then we talk about our days. I always keep in contact with my friends and we see each other and catch up whenever we have time.

Taha Hinengaro // Emotional and Mentall Wellbeing

To unwind and restore my mental energy I need to do something simple for myself. I love watching TV shows and reading books, so I'm going to keep up with that everyday. It feels like time invested in myself.

Taha Tihana // Spiritual Wellbeing

Through meditation I achieve a greater sense of spiritual wellbeing. I will do this every day to ensure that I stay well prepared and in tune with life.

Whenua // Connection to the Land

I walk my dog up through the trail near my house at least once or twice a week (my dad does the rest). I take time to take in the air and enjoy the views. I also do some bird watching on these walks.
